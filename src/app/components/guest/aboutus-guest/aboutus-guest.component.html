Hector Bonilla

Founder of BlueDrop

transforming the mexican agroforestry industry since 2000

BlueDrop was founded by Hector Bonilla in 2000 with the goal of developing high quality teak plantations in Mexico. In 2010 Proteak was taken public in the Mexican stocks market in the first IPO of a medium sized company and the first in the Mexican Forestry Sector. Proteak is, in 2020, the largest forestry company in Mexico with more than 20,000 hectares of teak and Eucalyptus plantations and the largest MDF factory in the country.

In 2017 we embarked in four more ventures under the leadership of Hector Bonilla and Franz Meiners. Bluedrop Berries, Bluedrop Produce, Bluedrop Nursery and Bluedrop Genetics.

Our strategy for the future is to continue growing in each of these lines of business leveraging the capabilities we have developed in the last 20 years.

2020 has provided no shortage of challenges but we will continue to invest in our companies and employees so that we can innovate and deliver our industry leading products to the world.
